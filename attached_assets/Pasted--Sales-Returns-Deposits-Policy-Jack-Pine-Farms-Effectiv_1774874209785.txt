## Sales / Returns / Deposits Policy (Jack Pine Farms)

Effective date: March 30, 2026

### 1) All sales are final

All purchases from Jack Pine Farms are final. We do not accept returns or offer refunds for any products once an order is placed, except where required by law.

### 2) Deposits are not refundable

Deposits are non-refundable. A deposit reserves product for a specific fulfillment window and helps us plan harvest/processing and inventory.

### 3) Pickup-only fulfillment (if this matches your current setup)

Orders are for local pickup only. Pickup instructions (date/time/location) will be provided at checkout and/or in your confirmation message.

### 4) No-shows and unclaimed orders

If an order is not picked up during the scheduled pickup window, we may be unable to hold product quality and safety. Unclaimed orders are not eligible for refund. If product is still safe and available, we may reschedule pickup at our discretion.

### 5) Problems with an order (quality or wrong item)

If you believe you received the wrong item or there is a quality issue, contact us within 24 hours of pickup with:

- Order name + order number (if available)
- A short description of the issue
- Photos (if applicable)

Even though sales are final, we may offer a replacement or store credit at our discretion when we can verify an issue originated on our side.

### 6) Cancellations / changes

Because our products are limited and planning-based, we may not be able to accommodate cancellations or changes after an order is placed. If you need help, contact us as soon as possible and we’ll do our best, but refunds are not available.
